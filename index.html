Sanol Book Series

Sanol Book Series takes you on an exhilarating journey through the eyes of Mukesh, a shopkeeper in Pune, who uncovers secrets in his barbershop while navigating the trials of life.

Written by Rian Jakhar and Suraj Bhatt, this captivating series blends joy, comedy, and suspense. Dive into the first installment and follow Mukesh as he strives to reunite with his mother, only to encounter unexpected twists.

The sequel, Sanol 2, introduces new characters and further intrigues. Join us in exploring this unique narrative that resonates with readers of all ages.
